The ThirdEye program is designed to power thriving companies and the growth-related needs of big enterprises who have a reputation to maintain, a customer base to satisfy, and a fierce competition in the industry.

Everything begins with a round-table discussion on the specific challenges of your industry and organization. Together, we can pinpoint the culprit responsible for slow growth, try proven solutions; and incorporate benchmarks to evaluate progress. On this program is a module for each need we might discover.

An external third eye is key! If you are one who works deep in your business, then your scope of view must be limited! This issue of perspective, as simple as it sounds, is the single reason why some companies crawl when others fly in the competition. And left unaddressed, entire businesses have been brought to an eventual halt!

But now, you are just one step away from the deep range of experience and the wide breadth of resources we offer as part of a multi-skilled company, with extra strategic partnerships. We will be bringing together all economically relevant professional competences, and the right combination of theory and practice. This outstanding program brought to your location can greatly improve your bottom line results.

Keep at the Top Of your Game!

What's your a pain as a medium/large-scale business?
Where would you want to change the game?
Or is it time to completely revamp and redefine your business?

This should be a matter between managers and decision makers, and the forward-thinking ThirdEye professionals - all on one round table.

Business and Process Upgrade

From a fresh, external, perspective, the ThirdEye program will scan your company in total - from management to workforce, from products to consumers - verifying that everything is on point. All behind closed doors. Then you are offered a targeted employee and organizational, and product development plan.

We could lighten your management costs by sifting out aging/outdated routines and unnecessary overheads accumulated overtime, save you a large amount from your old methods by deploying the right measure of today's technology, and increase productivity.

Truth is, there is no point running manual processes in a technology-driven world. See it the same as deciding to push a healthy car on the highway while others fly past it. But that would not be rational! Flying is what is!

Are you one of the decision makers? It may be time to upgrade to a company that's light, fast and ready to fly!

Product Innovation

Is your business/product secret of sometime ago still a secret? You can't be sure until serious market research is conducted. The ThirdEye will analyze your products and consumers, then measure this against the competition. Be prepared to change the game, because you are going to have to!

Steer clear of trial-and-error. Tap into our central database of what works and what doesn't - all built over time. Together, we can make everything feel brand new, while eagerly respecting current economic trends.

Get in touch for your product re-innovation!

Executive and Workforce Trainings

Provide your staff with the latest relevant knowledge and practices. Whether your goal is to develop staff, build leadership skills, or boost performance, we can help as a resource and partner.

We can improve your workforce and enhance your organization's performance in the areas critical to its success through education and training in information systems, programming, databases, operating systems, web technology, and ecommerce. Help your business keep up with emerging technologies!

Our custom programs are ideal for manager training, whether for first-time supervisors or executives. We will develop a highly-tuned training program for individuals, departments or all employees of your company, where necessary. We can tailor your program format, schedule, and location to fit your objectives and budget. You can choose between various formats ranging from short keynote speeches to comprehensive in-house programs.

Your company's progress depends on qualified executives and employees. And the primary mission of these trainings is to develop leaders who will be able to lead effectively now, and in the years to come.

Get in touch today for expert counsel on your staff's training needs.

Corporate Governance Improvement/Revolution

Management and corporate governance are the taproot to a business. The company's decision-making power and business secret lie here. Go wrong at the taproot and the whole trunk, branches, leaves and fruits will suffer. In fact, this has been a major reason some companies cannot make it anymore in business.

As managers, you have your business secrets. As the research-based ThirdEye, having the broadest view of the industry and a close interaction with your competitors, we have industry secrets. Our partnership together can align your business secret and decisions with industry standards and best practices, proven methods and emerging technologies.

We will partner with you behind the scene on a platform of research and insight that changes the way you think, approach management challenges, and make decisions. This will be an engine of action that alters the way you conduct business, measure results, and serve your community.

An external ThirdEye into management and governance maybe all you need to move forward, as every other thing may only be scratching the surface. With the ThirdEye, you'll develop your executives, clarify strategic vision and values, and create value for your organization and stakeholders.

Get in touch for a private conversation.
