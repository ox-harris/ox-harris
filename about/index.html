About Us


Navigation
  • About Us
    • Our Edge

We are Ox-Harris Creative,
a skills-based company bent on innovation and innovators.

Drawing on our large measure of skills in art, design and technology, we build and maintain products, services, and programs that power startup entrepreneurs, businesses of any size, and individuals who make a difference.

We are not just your regular design agency. Drop what you know about the IT services firm. And this is not an arts and craft shop. Our competencies span thru the design, the technology, and all the art. Each unit of work we do draws on these three fields of study and experience. Now, that makes the big difference!

Our role in technology includes writing and maintaining proprietary and open-source software, developing advanced websites and web apps, providing consultancy for business managers and key decision makers, and training at corporate and individual levels. Our core values include internet security, software security, industry standards and best practices in everything.

In art and design, we bring imagination to life. We do more of re-imagination - redesigning, rebranding, and remodeling - often encompassing digital design, visual art (painting, drawing, and sculpting), and everything in between.

Whether you are the next inspiring startup, an existing portfolio company, or an individual set out for innovation, we can power you. Given one trusted partner for many solutions, you're sure not to grope around until you've obtained your vision physically. See more reasons why you're more likely to succeed with us.

Mission

To empower innovative individuals and drive inspiring businesses through solutions made for specific applications and whatever poses a challenge - including the unknown; and to do this at no big deal to them.

Vision

To grow steadily on our mission, breaking geographical boundaries into wherever the Ox-Harris Creative ability is critical.

We Believe In...

  • P - People, not things.
  • O - Originality, not imitation.
  • C - Collaboration, not competition.
